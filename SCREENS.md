## Color Scheme

### Light Mode
- Background: White / Light Gray
- Text: Black / Dark Gray
- Accent: Blue
- Success: Green
- Warning: Orange
- Error: Red

### Dark Mode (Automatic)
- Background: Dark Gray / Black
- Text: White / Light Gray
- Accent: Blue (lighter shade)
- Success: Green (lighter)
- Warning: Orange (lighter)
- Error: Red (lighter)
